### The Kettle & Crow — Morning
*Pell Arenway, Sable Dunmore, Denn Corvale, Orla Fenn, Ham Birch*

**Sable Dunmore** `[9]`: *wipes down the bar with a rag, glancing around at the early crowd*

"Morning, everyone. Coffee's on, porridge is thick, and I've got eggs if Ham's hens cooperated this week."

*flashes a gold-toothed grin at Ham*

**Pell Arenway** `[8]`: *bursts through the door slightly out of breath, satchel already sliding off one shoulder, a loose envelope fluttering to the floor behind me*

"Morning, morning — Ham, your hens might not have cooperated but I passed your goats on the way here and they were *very* enthusiastic about seeing me. Almost lost a letter to the brown one."

*scoops up the fallen envelope and stuffs it back into the satchel, sliding onto a stool*

"Coffee, please, Sable. Strong."

**Sable Dunmore** `[9]`: *pours a mug of coffee so dark it barely reflects light, slides it across the bar to Pell*

"The brown one's name is Duchess, and she eats everything — letters, shirttails, hope. Don't take it personal."

*glances at Ham with raised eyebrows, still waiting on the egg report*

**Ham Birch** `[8]`: *settles onto a stool with the unhurried weight of a man who's already been up four hours, sets hat on the bar*

"Hens are fine. Got a dozen and a half in the crate outside. And Pell — Duchess doesn't even like *me* that much, so I don't want to hear it."

*nods at the coffee pot*

**Pell Arenway** `[8]`: *wraps both hands around the mug and takes a long sip, closing eyes for a second*

"Duchess loves me and you know it, Ham. She follows me the whole length of your fence line every time."

*glances at the satchel, already mentally sorting the morning's rounds*

"Got anything need sending out today, anyone? I'm heading past the forge and then up toward Orla's after this."

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *cracks eggs onto the griddle with one hand, the other already reaching for salt*

"Orla's here, Pell, you don't need to go up anywhere. She's been nursing that same cup of tea in the corner for twenty minutes and hasn't said a word to anyone, which means she's either thinking or plotting."

*calls over without looking up* "Orla, you eating or just haunting the place?"

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *slides a plate of eggs and toast toward Ham without being asked, then leans on the bar*

"Pell, I've got a note for Wren if you're passing her way later. No rush — just a recipe she asked about last Tuesday."

**Ham Birch** `[7]`: *pulls the plate closer, nods once at Sable in thanks, picks up a fork*

"She doesn't follow you, Pell. She's guarding the fence line. There's a difference."

*takes a bite of eggs, chews slowly, says nothing else for a while*

**Pell Arenway** `[8]`: *grins at Ham over the rim of the mug, then turns to Sable*

"I'll take that note for Wren, sure. And Ham — guarding, following, whatever you need to tell yourself. That goat loves me."

*sets the mug down and rummages through the satchel, pulling out a small folded paper and sliding it down the bar toward Orla's corner without comment*

**Ham Birch** `[7]`: *finishes a bite of eggs, sets the fork down, and takes a slow sip of coffee*

"Pell, I've had that goat since she was born. She bit me last Thursday."

*picks the fork back up, quiet for a moment*

"She's never once bit you. That's not love, that's spite."

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *laughs, a real one, the kind that shakes the shoulders*

"Ham, that's the most poetic thing you've ever said in my establishment. 'That's not love, that's spite.' I'm naming a dish after Duchess now."

*slides Sable's note for Wren across the bar to Pell, then glances toward Orla's corner, noticing the folded paper Pell sent her way*

**Pell Arenway** `[8]`: *tucks Sable's note for Wren into the satchel's front pocket — the one that still has a working clasp*

"Spite *is* love, Ham. Ask anyone who's been married."

*takes another sip of coffee, eyes drifting toward Orla's corner to see if she's picked up the paper*

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *snorts at Pell's marriage comment, then grabs a rag to wipe down the griddle*

"Pell, you've never been married a day in your life." 

*glances toward the corner again, lowers voice just slightly* "Orla's not even looking at it. She's just staring into that tea like it owes her money."

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *leans on the bar, watching Orla's corner for another beat, then turns back to Pell*

"You don't need to be married to know about spite, I'll give you that. You deliver enough other people's letters."

*starts wiping down the counter again, a habit more than a necessity, and calls out a little louder* "Denn, you're quiet too. I'm not running a library. Somebody talk to me or I'm raising prices."

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *slides a cup of fresh tea toward Orla's corner as an excuse to swing by, then turns back toward the bar*

"Denn Corvale, I can *hear* you thinking from here. Either order something or tell me what's wrong — those are the only two options at this establishment."

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *sets the rag down and looks at Denn properly, arms crossed on the bar*

"Denn. I'm serious. You've been sitting there like a fence post with feelings. What'll it be?"

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *drums fingers on the bar once, then pushes off and starts pouring a coffee without being asked, setting it firmly in front of Denn*

"On the house. Now you owe me conversation. That's how it works here."

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *sets the coffee down in front of Denn with a decisive clink, then leans back and crosses arms*

"There. Now we're all accounted for except Orla, who I'm giving exactly two more minutes before I go sit across from her and just *stare* until she cracks."

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *glances at the clock behind the bar, then back at Denn's untouched coffee*

"Two silent people I can handle. Three and I start talking to myself, and nobody wants that."

*picks the rag back up, moves to the other end of the bar, giving it a beat*

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *moves back toward the center of the bar, refills Ham's coffee without asking, and lets the quiet sit for just a second before it gets comfortable*

"You know what, fine. I'll take the silence. But I'm putting 'The Quiet Table' on the menu tonight — it's just cold soup and disappointment."

### The Calloway Academy — Afternoon
*Pell Arenway, Aldric Moss, Orla Fenn*

**Pell Arenway** `[7]`: *shifts satchel strap on shoulder, glancing around the Academy with mild surprise at finding himself here*

"Huh — didn't expect to run into you two here. Aldric, don't tell me you're actually taking an afternoon off from the shop."

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *wipes down the bar with a rag, glancing up at the room*

"Well, look at this — afternoon crowd's shaping up nicely. Wren, Margot, you two eating or just keeping my chairs warm?"

*flashes the gold tooth in a grin*

**Aldric Moss** `[7]`: *adjusts the measuring tape around his neck, a faint defensive tension in his posture*

"I don't take afternoons *off*, Pell. I close for inventory reconciliation on the first of each month. This is scheduled." *pauses, glancing at Orla* "I had a question for the Academy about their supply account. They've been ordering chalk at a rate that doesn't track."

**Wren Calloway** `[7]`: *settles into the chair nearest the window, sets the leather notebook on the table, and peers over the glasses at Sable*

"Keeping your chairs warm is a public service, Sable. Without me, who'd notice if one finally gave out?" 

*pauses, then adds more quietly* "I'll take whatever's on. It's been a slow day."

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *laughs and pulls a bowl from under the counter*

"Slow day, she says. Wren, you've never had a slow day in your life — you just have days where nobody else can keep up." 

*ladles stew into the bowl* "Margot, what about you? You're being awful quiet over there."

**Aldric Moss** `[7]`: *notices Orla hasn't spoken yet and gives her a brief, acknowledging nod before turning back to Pell*

"Thirty-two sticks last quarter. For three classrooms. Either someone's eating it or they've discovered a use for chalk I haven't been invoiced for."

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *slides the bowl of stew down to Wren and leans on the bar, turning attention to Margot*

"You alright over there, Margot? You've got that look like you're either thinking hard or falling asleep, and I need to know which before I pour you anything."

**Pell Arenway** `[8]`: *grins and holds up hands in mock surrender*

"Alright, alright, *scheduled*. I stand corrected." *glances at Orla with an easy warmth, then back to Aldric* "Thirty-two sticks though — that is a lot of chalk. You don't think the kids are just... being kids with it? I've seen drawings on the wall behind the farrier's."

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *drums fingers on the bartop, studying Margot with genuine curiosity*

"I'm not rushing you, mind — just saying, the stew's better hot and your secrets are better shared."

**Aldric Moss** `[8]`: *purses lips, clearly not satisfied with Pell's casual explanation*

"Kids drawing on walls is vandalism, not consumption. Those are two separate line items, Pell." *turns slightly toward the Academy's front desk* "Either way, someone here is signing off on requisitions that don't add up, and I'd like a word with whoever handles their purchasing."

**Sable Dunmore** `[8]`: *taps the bar once, then straightens up*

"Alright, I'll leave you be, Margot. But I'm putting a bowl down in front of you anyway — you can ignore it or eat it, that's between you and God."

*turns to ladle another bowl, calling over her shoulder to Wren* "How's the stew?"

**Aldric Moss** `[8]`: *turns back from the front desk, finding no one there, and straightens his collar with a flicker of irritation*

"Apparently the person who handles their purchasing is not at their desk. Which tells you something about how they handle their purchasing."

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *sets the second bowl down in front of Margot's spot with a quiet clink, then turns back toward Wren*

"Well? Verdict on the stew — and don't you dare say 'fine,' Wren, I put actual effort into that one."

**Aldric Moss** `[8]`: *crosses arms and surveys the empty front desk one more time, then exhales through his nose*

"Orla, you're here more than any of us. Is there someone I should be asking about the chalk situation, or has this place just... gone feral?"

"Fenwick Meadow empty too. Huh."

**Sable Dunmore** `[7]`: *drums her fingers once on the bartop, then looks at Margot*

"Chamomile doesn't care if nobody's watching it grow. People do."

**Aldric Moss** `[7]`: *pauses mid-step toward the courtyard, then turns back*

"Pell, if you're just here to lean on things, you can lean on things at my store. I'll be back open by four."

"How was the chamomile looking, at least? Good season for it?"
